Welcome to my website :) ▌

"Nostalgia is a hell of a drug".

I enjoy things that remind me of simpler times.

As a result, I chose to revamp my personal website to reflect my personality and the things that I enjoy. Instead of having a fancy yet generic templated site with smooth Vue3 components, I preferred a humble and minimal page with old school marquee tags and GIF backgrounds. It ain't much but I'd rather have it this way.

My mom first made me an email account for my 10th birthday back in 2008 and I used it as frequently as I use my phone now, almost 15 years later.

I am eternally grateful to my parents for giving me an environment where I could play around on the internet since a huge amount of people still don't have that luxury.
